Hiking in NC

There are many great hiking locations across the state of North Carolina.

Many of these locations can be accessed via the beautiful Blue Ridge Parkway.

Some of the best areas to hike include:

  1. Boone
  2. Asheville
  3. Linville

A few of my favorite hikes in and around North Carolina include:

  • Grandfather Mountain
  • Roan Mountain
  • Grayson Highlands

Grayson Highlands

The Grayson Highlands are part of the Appalachian Trail and feature wild ponies.
